Our Mission

No kid should ever go hungry. Every single day, millions of children around the world go without food. Here at Luncher, we aim to solve this problem by providing a platform for schools to broadcast the needs of their students. School administrators are able to update their funding needs, showing exactly how much money the school needs to provide food for each every student. Community members, like you, are able to view a list of schools requesting financial support to help provide food for their students. No amount of money is too little and all proceeds go directly to the school.
